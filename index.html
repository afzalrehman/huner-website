ABOUT THE HUNAR FOUNDATION (THF)

Registered in 2008 under the Societies Act 1860, THF took its first batch in January 2010 within the premises provided by Delhi Mercantile Society, Karachi in its DMS School facility. The students gave their exam at the end of the year and till 2021, 15 batches have graduated in 6 months to 1 year diploma courses.

THF has added its campuses in:

  • Rashidabad, Tando Allahyar
  • Bedian Road, Lahore
  • Pak Arab Fertilizer, Multan
  • GT Road, Kharian
  • Saddar, Karachi

In 2020 our Flagship Korangi Complex came into existence having Teachers Training Center, male and female institutes offering Health Alliances to Emerging Technology Programs, Call Center Training and various other courses.

WORKING TOWARDS A SKILLED PAKISTAN

At present, THF has eight operational institutes across Pakistan which produced 8000 skilled graduates (Boys and Girls) since 2010. With more institutes to become operational in upcoming months, THF plans to increase the number of purpose built Vocational & Technical Institutes.

VISION

Creating a new cadre of skilled Pakistanis with internationally recognized technical qualifications, opening up a new world of opportunities. i.e. A SKILLED PAKISTAN

MISSION

To initiate a movement to skill the motivated illiterate, semi-literate and literate: To provide technical training ‘with a difference’ in quality of skills as well as ethical, social and moral values.
